# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to creating a welcoming, respectful, and collaborative environment for everyone.

Participation in this project should be a positive experience regardless of experience level, gender, identity, disability, ethnicity, religion or nationality.

---

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful.
- Accept constructive feedback.
- Help other contributors learn.
- Assume good intentions.
- Focus discussions on improving the project.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination.
- Personal attacks.
- Offensive language.
- Publishing private information.
- Any behavior that negatively impacts the community.

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, contributions or participants that violate this Code of Conduct.

---

Thank you for helping make Inbox Guardian a welcoming project.
